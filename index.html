WHAT WE OFFER

Fertility Consultation & Evaluation

We begin with a thorough assessment of your reproductive health, understanding your unique medical history. Our specialists conduct detailed diagnostic tests to identify the root cause of infertility. Based on these insights, we design a personalized roadmap tailored to your needs. We ensure you feel supported, informed, and confident at every step. Your journey to parenthood starts with clarity and care.

IUI, IVF, ICSI, IMSI

We offer a full spectrum of advanced fertility treatments designed for high success rates. From Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) to complex In Vitro Fertilization (IVF), we use world-class protocols. Our specialized ICSI and IMSI techniques address male fertility challenges with precision. Every procedure is performed in our state-of-the-art labs by experienced embryologists. We are committed to realizing your dream of having a healthy baby.

Advanced Embryo Monitoring (Embryoscope)

Our facility is equipped with the revolutionary Embryoscope technology for continuous monitoring. This allows us to observe embryo development in real-time without removing them from the incubator. By selecting the healthiest embryos with the highest implantation potential, we significantly improve pregnancy rates. This non-invasive, precise approach ensures the best start for your future child. Technology meets care to deliver superior outcomes.

Egg & Embryo Freezing

Whether for medical reasons or social planning, we help you preserve your fertility for the future. Our advanced vitrification (rapid freezing) techniques ensure high survival rates for eggs and embryos. This empowers women to delay motherhood without compromising on the quality of their eggs. Couples can also store excess embryos for future siblings. Your biological clock doesn't have to dictate your life choices.

Recurrent Implantation Failure Testing

For couples who have faced repeated setbacks, we offer specialized investigation and care. We analyze genetic, immunological, and uterine factors that may affect implantation. Our experts implement personalized protocols to address these specific challenges effectively. We don't just repeat the process; we refine it with medical precision. We are dedicated to turning past failures into future success.

Women's Health & Gynaecology Services

Beyond fertility, we provide comprehensive healthcare for women at every stage of life. Our services range from routine check-ups and menstrual health management to complex gynaecological surgeries. We focus on preventive care, hormonal balance, and overall wellness. Our compassionate team ensures a comfortable and private environment for all your needs. We are your partners in long-term health and well-being.
